The Problem

Growing Families Need
Room to Grow

Greenpoint has always been a neighborhood where families put down roots. That's part of what makes our community special.

But Greenpoint is growing — more housing, more families, and more demand for early childhood education. Local providers are seeing it every day through increased applications and longer waitlists.

There is a clear gap between demand and available Universal 3K in the neighborhood. Without action, families will be forced to leave, inequality will widen, and kids will miss out on critical early learning years.

Without Universal Childcare, families spend $20,000–$30,000 a year on childcare — money that would otherwise drive economic growth right here in Greenpoint.
